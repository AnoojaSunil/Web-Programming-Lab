Meals

  • Early Morning
    • 6.00 am
    After waking up at 6 am have a cup warm water with juice of half a lime and honey
  • Breakfast
    • 7.00 am
    After that we can have our breakfast with 3 iddlis and small bowl sambar or 2 egg whites,1/2 a small bowl of peanut and coconut chutney and 1 cup green tea
  • Mid Morning
    • 10.00 am
    At 10 am it is good to eat 1 medium apple or a small bowl of grapes
  • Lunch
    • 12.30 to 1.00 pm
    For the lunch we can have a bowl of rice,vegetable curry,1 cup rasam and 1cup buttermilk
  • Post-Lunch
    • 3.30 to 4.00 pm
    During the evening drink a cup of green tea with 2 marie biscuits
  • Dinner
    • 7.00 pm
    At night it is better to have 2 roties,1 medium bowl of vegetable curry,1 cup spinach daal and 1 small bowl of curd
  • Bed time
    • 10.00 pm
    Before going to sleep drink a cup of warm milk with a pinch of turmeric
